meet


Certain posets X have a binary operation meet denoted by ∧, such that x∧y is the greatest lower bound of x and y. Such posets are called meet-semilattices, or ∧-semilattices, or lower semilattices.

If m and m′ are both meets of x and y, then m≤m′ and m≥m′, and so m=m′; thus a meet, if it exists, is unique. The meet is also known as the and operator.
