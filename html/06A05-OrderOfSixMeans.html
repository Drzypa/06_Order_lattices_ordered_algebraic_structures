order of six means


The of the six usual means of two positive numbers (a and b) is from the least to the greatest one

  1. 1.

    harmonic mean,

  2. 2.

    geometric mean,

  3. 3.

    Heronian mean,

  4. 4.

    arithmetic mean,

  5. 5.

    quadratic mean,

  6. 6.

    contraharmonic mean,
